--- Day 19: Grid Traversal ---

Part 1

There's a grid of digits ranging from 1 to 9.

You're in charge of controlling a robot. This robot begins in the top left and ends in the bottom right. In addition, this robot can only move 1 cell downwards or 1 cell rightwards.

For a single grid, you score points equal to the sum of the digits on the grid that you traverse. Determine the highest score you can achieve!

The input consists of 5 square grids, your overall score is the product of each grid's score!

Example Input:

19532
36182
93847
85364
17385

123
456
789

The highest achievable score is 50 on the first grid and 29 on the second grid, as follows:

19532
36182
93847
85364
17385

123
456
789

The overall score is 50 √ó 29 = 1450.

What is the highest score you can achieve?
