--- Day 20: Sprinklers ---

Part 1

Today, your boss has tasked you with investigating their sprinkler system.

Each sprinkler is located at an integer coordinate (x, y) and covers a circular range with radius r.

Two sprinklers are considered "overlapping" if the range they cover shares a positive amount of area. It is guaranteed no two ranges are tangent.

Find the sprinkler that's overlapping with the most other sprinklers! If this sprinkler is located at (x, y) and overlaps with z other sprinklers, report the answer as x * y + z

Example Input:

(4, 6) r=3
(3, 7) r=1
(12, 14) r=9
(10, 6) r=5

The sprinkler at (4, 6) overlaps with 3 other sprinklers, the most of any sprinkler.

Hence, the final answer is 4 * 6 + 3 = 27

What is the sprinkler that overlaps with the most other sprinklers in your input?
