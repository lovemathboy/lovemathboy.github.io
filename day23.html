--- Day 23: Grid Traversal v3 ---

Part 1

Remember the robots from Day 19: Grid Traversal? They're back!

With a brand new software update, they can now travel in all 4 cardinal directions! (Unfortunately still only 1 unit at a time)

The top left cell is an 'S', denoting the start. The bottom right cell is an 'E', denoting the end.

Also unlike last time, you want to minimize your score - the sum of digits you travel across.

There are multiple square grids in the input, your final score is the product of all grids!

Example Input:

S2121
99925
52219
11997
3212E

S23
456
78E

The minimum score for each grid is 18 and 11 respectively, as follows:

S2121
99925
52219
11997
3212E

S23
456
78E

The final score is 18 * 11 = 198

What is the product of the minimum scores of all the square grids in your input?
