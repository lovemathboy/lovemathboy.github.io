--- Day 16: Remedial Lessons ---

Part 1

Due to your immense Part 2 screw-ups in the past few days, your boss has sent you for remedial lessons.

Each lesson occurs exactly once each week. Every week, the lesson has the same start time and end time.

Obviously, you cannot attend two classes at the same time. However, since teleportation was conveniently invented 5 seconds ago, if one class ends at the same time as another class starts, you can still take both classes.

Your task is to determine the maximum number of remedial lessons you can attend in one week.

Example Input:

Lesson #1: Starts at t = 0 and ends at t = 20
Lesson #2: Starts at t = 50 and ends at t = 150
Lesson #3: Starts at t = 180 and ends at t = 200
Lesson #4: Starts at t = 190 and ends at t = 240 
Lesson #5: Starts at t = 10 and ends at t = 40
Lesson #6: Starts at t = 30 and ends at t = 170
Lesson #7: Starts at t = 160 and ends at t = 190

The maximum number of classes you can attend is 4.
One possible way is to attend Lesson #1, Lesson #2, Lesson #7, and Lesson #4 in that order.
Notice that Lesson #7 ends exactly when Lesson #4 starts. Due to the invention to teleportation, you can attend both!

What is the maximum number of lessons you can attend?
